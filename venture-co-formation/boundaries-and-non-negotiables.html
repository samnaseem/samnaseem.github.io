Boundaries & Non Negotiables

Absolute constraints governing Venture Co Formation

This page defines the absolute constraints governing Venture Co-Formation.

They are not starting points for discussion.

They are the conditions under which discussion is allowed.

Protection Is Mandatory

No architectural discussion, system design, or feasibility exploration occurs without a pre-engagement agreement in place.

This requirement is non-negotiable.

Until protection exists:

  • no concepts are evaluated
  • no systems are framed
  • no assumptions are explored
  • no feasibility is assessed

Conversation without protection is considered exposure.

Intellectual Property Principles

The following principles apply at all times:

  • architectural concepts remain protected unless explicitly assigned
  • derivative works are governed by agreement
  • no implied licence exists through conversation
  • silence does not imply consent
  • withdrawal preserves intellectual property protection

These principles apply regardless of whether a venture proceeds.

No Informal Engagement

The following are explicitly out of scope:

  • “just helping you think”
  • informal whiteboarding
  • exploratory calls without consequence
  • unpaid discovery or validation
  • parallel use of ideas outside this channel

If protection is bypassed at any point, activity stops immediately.

No Obligation to Proceed

Participation in this channel creates:

  • no commitment to form a venture
  • no obligation to continue discussion
  • no expectation of further involvement

Declining to proceed is considered a valid and successful outcome.

Authority and Ownership Alignment

The following constraints are absolute:

  • no dilution of architectural authority without compensation
  • no assumption of execution without governance position
  • no separation of long-term risk from decision-making authority
  • no retroactive negotiation of ownership

Equality of effort does not imply equality of ownership.

Time and Scope Constraints

Exploration within this channel is:

  • time-bounded
  • consequence-aware
  • non-iterative by default

Repeated exploration without commitment is not supported.

Structural Integrity Over Rescue

This channel does not exist to:

  • rescue structurally unsound ventures
  • compensate for missing fundamentals
  • absorb risk without authority
  • correct misaligned founding decisions

If structural integrity cannot be achieved, disengagement is the correct outcome.

Enforcement

These boundaries are enforced consistently.

If any boundary is challenged, activity pauses until alignment is restored or engagement ends.

This is not adversarial.

It is protective.
