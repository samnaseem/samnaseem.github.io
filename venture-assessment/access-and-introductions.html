Access & Introductions

How access occurs

How access occurs

The Venture Assessment Channel has no open entry.

Access occurs only through:

  • personal introduction,
  • trusted referral,
  • or an existing relationship.

This is intentional.

No open submissions

There is no mechanism to:

  • submit ideas,
  • send pitch decks,
  • request assessment,
  • or initiate engagement through this site.

Unsolicited approaches are declined by default.

Silence should not be interpreted as feedback.

Role of introducers

Introductions made through this channel carry implicit responsibility.

Introducers are expected to:

  • understand the boundaries of this channel,
  • avoid positioning it as a service or opportunity,
  • and ensure basic alignment before making contact.

Introductions that bypass these expectations may not proceed.

Pre-engagement requirement

No substantive discussion occurs without a pre-engagement agreement in place.

This agreement exists to:

  • protect intellectual contribution,
  • define boundaries,
  • establish asymmetry explicitly,
  • and prevent future disputes.

Surface-level context may be exchanged prior to agreement.

Assessment does not begin.

What happens after introduction

Following a valid introduction:

  • an initial framing conversation may occur,
  • key assumptions and risks are surfaced,
  • and a decision is made whether to proceed with assessment.

There is no obligation to continue.

A decision not to proceed is a complete outcome.

No persistence, no escalation

Repeated follow-up, escalation through intermediaries, or attempts to reopen a closed decision are not effective.

Decisions are not softened over time.

Why access is constrained

Access is constrained to:

  • protect time and attention,
  • preserve judgement quality,
  • and ensure that assessment remains deliberate rather than reactive.

Constraint is a feature of this channel, not a limitation.

Final note

If access to this channel feels difficult, that is intentional.

It exists to ensure that architectural judgement and long-term responsibility are deployed only where they are justified.

Saying no is the default.

Saying yes is consequential.

End of core context.
